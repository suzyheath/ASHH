We are all living such crazy busy lives...but what happens if we hit the pause button? What happens if we escape the city streets with our feet still on the ground?

Let's find out ... let's get wild!

At Let's Get Wild, we're passionate about living fast and free in nature's playground. To celebrate the freedom of our favourite sports, we curate an exciting programme of the best sports videography out there. The previous festival programmes have showed a diverse range of approaches to extreme sport representation, including feature and 3D films. Indulge in a selection of independent cinema.
